Moon Marbles is a P2E endless runner game. You play as a moon, rolling on planets trying to escape from the resident aliens.

You'll need to dodge meteors, craters, obstacles, and of course...aliens in this endless running game.

Collect moon coins to unlock power-ups and special gear to help you go further every time in Moon Marbles. Furthermore, coins can be used to purchase gear to assist you through the levels.

With future cosmic morphing you can upgrade your moons with permanent gear to assist you through levels every time you play.
