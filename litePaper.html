Abstract
Welcome to the new era of online, decentralized gaming where you own your own properties, governed by the community and supported by a complex of the economy where supply and demand are the greatest currency through a fun in-game style with a rewarding utility token.

Moon Marbles! Our P2E (Play to Earn) Marble Game rewards players to conquer levels with a rewarding system to the players in form of our decentralized utility native token.
The introduction to blockchain technology is assisting in the advancemnet of game production. Blockchains wonderful properties, such as decentralization, security, transparency, and freedom can let traditional games do things that were previously impossible.

It has been a short but amazing road to where we are now. We're ecstatic to be working on a game that will bring joy to people all accross the world. Games, we believe, are an essential aspect of human existence. it allows you to discover people's, groups', and civilizations' latent skills that are limited or unachieveable in real life.
The Problem
Moon Marbles recognizes P2E games have limited potential of earnings upon how many assets a player may have. Ths makes it hard for the player with a small amount of ETH to be competitive. How many P2E Games have you seen allowing the "Small Fish" able to enlarge their financial position just by sheer gaming skill?
The Solution
Our game allows even the smallest players to ourpace their competition and earn big by beating levels with skill. Along with skill, players can purchase one-time use items to help propel them through the levels while they earn. Each marble will have the different gear to help them through the game limiting their need to buy items.

  • Advantage: Some marbles will eventually have gear equipped to them 24/7 offering an advantage on overall game difficulty.
  • Convenience: Gear and shop items make the levels easier to get through earning Moon Tokens along the way.
  • Equipped: Depending on your gear you will be able to show off your marble after "The Morphing" reducing supply by up to 90%.
  • Story: Each item and level will unfold a storyline. One that unravels slowly greatly rewarding those who show the most patience.
